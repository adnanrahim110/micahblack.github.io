Lessons from a Life on the Edge

Linked Recoveries is a candid, adrenaline-fueled memoir of a life lived on the edge—both of mountains and mental health. Micah Black takes readers through a whirlwind of skiing fame, extreme adventures, and personal darkness. Meet Micah Black

Micah Black grew up with skis on his feet and adventure in his soul. A pioneer of freeriding, he rose to fame through bold descents and a fearless attitude. But his greatest accomplishment? Finding freedom within.

Micah Black is more than a skiing legend—he’s a storyteller, a survivor, and a symbol of resilience. Through his incredible journey, he reminds us that every fall is an opportunity to rise.

As a pivotal figure in Teton Gravity Research, he showcased the untamed beauty of the mountains while living a rockstar life behind the scenes. But the fall from the peak was steep. Micah’s post-stardom struggles with depression and addiction led him to rebuild his life and find new meaning in recovery. His story isn’t just about skiing—it’s about the resilience of the human spirit.
